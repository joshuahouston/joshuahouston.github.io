A Tale of Mark Watney's Survival on Mars

Sol 6 found Mark Watney all alone on the red planet after a violent dust storm forced the astronauts of Ares 3 to abort their month-long mission. En route to the escape vehicle, the MAV (Mars ascent vehicle), an antenna from the communications set-up broke off and struck Watney, puncturing his suit and knocking him out of sight from the others. The bio-sensors of Watney's suit indicated to the other crew a loss of all vitals. They were forced to leave him behind rather than risk the entire crew to recover his body.

Sols 6 through 69

Mark Watney was not ready to die on Mars. Grow a farm. Find a way to contact Earth. From the safety of the Hab, Watney made the most of every resource and chemical he had available to him. He made use of his own recyclables to fertilize and mix with Martian soil and started a potato farm. He ignited hydrogen and oxygen in the air to make water.

Sirius Missions
