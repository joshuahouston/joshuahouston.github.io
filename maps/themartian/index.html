A Tale of Mark Watney's Survival on Mars

Sol 6 found Mark Watney all alone on the red planet after a violent dust storm forced the astronauts of Ares 3 to abort their month-long mission. En route to board the MAV (Mars ascent vehicle), an antennae struck Watney, puncturing his suit and knocking him out of view from the others. The bio-sensors of Watney's suit indicated to the other crew a loss of all vitals. They were forced to leave him behind rather than risk the entire crew to recover his body.
