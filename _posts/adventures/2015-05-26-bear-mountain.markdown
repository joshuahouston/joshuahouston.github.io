Do *not* call Bear Mountain "Easy peasy"
![tent][id]
Yes, it's beautiful, but it wasn't easy getting here, or getting down.

I had climbed Bear Mountain before but went up a different way. This new way did not have a sketchy snow-field crossing, it just had a sketchy 3,000 feet of avoiding cliffs and ravines, and climbing through alders, dogwood thickets, entangling stunted Sitka Spruce, and many other fun nature things.
