I wanted to do something with this cool old aerial I came across at work. I decided to use the Mapbox GL feature of comparing maps. 
I did this once before with a simple Leaflet OSM map but it was limited to North being up. With Mapbox GL I can specify a bearing, or twist, for the map. This allows me to align the view with the dimensions of the aerial photo.
